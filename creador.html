FIVE NIGHTS AT FREDDY'S

CREADOR

Scott Braden Cawthon (born July 26, 1971) is an American video game developer, writer, and producer. He is best known for creating the Five Nights at Freddy’s franchise, which began with the development of the eponymous game in 2014. Released independently, the game quickly gained popularity and achieved cult status. Cawthon developed a total of 10 games in the series from 2014 until his retirement in 2021, along with three spin-offs. He also wrote several stories based on Five Nights at Freddy’s, including the novel The Silver Eyes and an upcoming movie
