FIVE NIGHT AT FREDDY'S

INICIO

Welcome to the Five Nights at Freddy's Wiki, the comprehensive encyclopedia site about Five Nights at Freddy's, a media franchise based around an indie point-and-click horror video game series created by Scott Cawthon. The series is centered on the universe of various fictional restaurant chains (pastiches of real-life restaurants such as Chuck E. Cheese's and ShowBiz Pizza Place) hosted by the Fazbear Entertainment company. Join the collaborative community to detect recent information and announcements with us freely.
